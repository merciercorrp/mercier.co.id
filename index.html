About

Mercier Corp

Mercier Corp is a beauty company focused on innovation, quality, and sustainability. We offer premium skincare products, professional beauty clinic services, and cosmetic manufacturing. Committed to balancing profit, human well-being, and environmental impact, we aim to provide beauty solutions that benefit both customers and the world responsibly.

We hold the principle that every innovative idea and every solution-driven approach can inspire and open new opportunities for a better future.

Yunna Mercier

Founder of Mercier Corp

Mercier


Mercier Corp has over 1,000 employees, whom we refer to as Mercier Partners, spread across 38 regions. They play a key role in creating high-quality beauty products and providing the best solutions to meet customer needs across Indonesia.
